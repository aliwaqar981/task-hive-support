Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new task?

To create a new task, go to the main dashboard and click on the 'New Task' button. Fill in the required details and save.

How can I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on 'Forgot Password'. Follow the instructions sent to your email.
